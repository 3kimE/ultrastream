## Channel Logos

Create SVG or PNG channel logos at `public/channels/{channel-id}.svg`.
Size: 200×200px, transparent background.

Currently using colored circle placeholders with initials. Replace with real logos when you have licensing rights.

**Priority channels to replace:**
- ESPN, Sky Sports, beIN Sports, HBO, CNN, BBC News, Al Jazeera
- Nickelodeon, Disney Junior, Cartoon Network
- Nat Geo, Discovery, History Channel

**File naming:** lowercase, hyphenated (e.g., `espn.svg`, `sky-sports.svg`, `bein-sports.svg`)

> ⚠️ Do NOT use official broadcast logos without licensing permission.
> Use a professional logo provider like Brandfetch for licensed usage.
